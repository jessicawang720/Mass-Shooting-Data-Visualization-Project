  There are some of the relevant research has already been completed on mass shootings such as mass shooting with media coverage; mass shootings and mental illness; mass shootings and psychotropic drugs. In my dataset, I want to learn which national trends influence the mass shooting most, and I want to figure out how to minimize the number of mass shooting. I try to use EDA process to explore, learn about and get an outline of the dataset, and even to find out whether there is other information I need to complete the research. At first, I try to find the pattern of mass shooting and find out the safest place to live in, but I cannot make any prediction from the results. Then I decide to find the national trends which contribute the most to the number of mass shootings. I developed my datasets and come out my final question: how to minimize the number of total victims in the mass shooting?
